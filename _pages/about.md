I am an associate professor at the School of Artificial Intelligence at [Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications](https://www.bupt.edu.cn). Before joining BUPT, I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences and conducted postdoctoral research at Indiana University.

My research interests include cognitive neuroscience and brain imaging-based analysis of development and function. The work primarily utilizes brain magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) to investigate human brain network connectivity and its developmental patterns; the research techniques involve neuroimaging data processing and the analysis of human brain network connectivity based on complex network theory,and also trying to apply the methodology to study atypical development of brain which causes mental disorders, like depression and autism disorder..
